FAQ

Common questions before you start.

Do I need Kite to use Gains?

No. Kite is optional and only enhances portfolio-aware insights. Core stock analysis still works without it.

Is the app usable before full Firebase and Postgres setup?

Yes. The current flow supports local sessions and local storage fallback until those integrations are added.

What makes this feel different from a generic screener?

The product is optimized around decision framing, comparison, source visibility, and reasoning-backed outputs instead of raw data dumps.
